Our Services

Waste Audit

At TrashTrek Eco Solutions, we conduct comprehensive waste audits to analyze waste generation patterns and existing management systems. Whether it's a small residential complex or a large industrial premises, our audits, typically spanning 2 to 7 days, provide valuable insights. Following the audit, we deliver a detailed report with recommendations for optimal wet and dry waste processing solutions.

Consultation and Technical Guidance

We offer expert consultation and technical guidance to housing societies, corporates, civic bodies, and CSR clients in establishing and optimizing waste management systems. Our approach involves designing customized decentralized solutions tailored to each client's waste generation patterns, site-specific conditions, and preferences. We recommend appropriate composting systems such as manual tumblers, OWC machines, compost curing systems, and biogas plants to suit their needs.

Operation and Maintenance Services

TrashTrek Eco Solutions provides operation and maintenance services for waste recycling and processing facilities on a contract basis. Our dedicated team ensures seamless daily operations, including segregation, door-to-door collection, composting, and waste recycling. Transparency is paramount in our operations, maintained through rigorous supervision, monitoring, reporting, and data representation.

Revival of Existing Composting Systems

We specialize in reviving existing composting systems by offering repair services and comprehensive AMC contracts. Whether it's enhancing efficiency or extending the lifespan of composting infrastructure, we ensure your systems remain operational and effective.

Educational Visits and Knowledge Sharing

As part of our commitment to promoting awareness about waste management, TrashTrek Eco Solutions conducts educational visits for students, organizations, and institutes. Our packages include introductory sessions and site visits, providing participants with valuable insights into sustainable waste management practices.

Awareness Sessions and Interactive Events

We organize engaging awareness sessions and interactive events for schools, colleges, communities, corporates, and housing societies. Through various approaches such as street plays, training sessions, and Q&A sessions tailored to diverse audiences, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of waste management and inspire positive action.
