Creating smarter solutions for a
better tomorrow

TrashTrek Eco Solutions is a pioneering social enterprise dedicated to revolutionizing waste management practices in Tamil Nadu, with a special focus on The Nilgiris District. Founded by a team of passionate individuals with diverse expertise from Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) Hyderabad, our mission is to disrupt the linear model of waste management prevalent today, while contributing to the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

TrashTrek Eco Solutions envisions a world where waste is transformed into valuable resources without compromising the well-being of people or the planet. Our commitment lies in offering innovative waste solutions tailored to meet the evolving needs of our society, particularly in ecologically sensitive areas like The Nilgiris, aligning with several SDGs including SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), and SDG 13 (Climate Action).

Our Approach and Services

At TrashTrek, we embrace a holistic approach, prioritizing environmental sustainability, engagement with informal sectors, and economic viability. Our comprehensive services encompass every aspect of decentralized waste management, from initial waste audits to the design, implementation, and ongoing maintenance of waste handling systems.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction. We don't just provide waste management solutions; we offer personalized guidance to help our clients achieve their sustainability goals, including the ambitious target of zero waste.

Our esteemed clientele includes a wide spectrum of organizations, ranging from townships and corporations to educational institutions and municipal bodies in The Nilgiris District and beyond.

Looking ahead, TrashTrek Eco Solutions is committed to scaling our impact beyond The Nilgiris. Through incubation and franchise development initiatives, we aim to replicate our successful model in other regions of India and potentially across the developing world, while ensuring our efforts align with the unique ecological challenges of ecologically sensitive areas.
